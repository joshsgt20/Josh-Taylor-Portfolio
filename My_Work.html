Social Media Marketing

At University, I specialised in social media marketing / campaigning - learning the theory behind social media and its massive influence in todays society, whilst also being able to gain experience by completing multiple online campaigns different organisations, to either help raise awareness or to promote events.

These campaigns have always been completed independently and involve a high amount of planning to be complete. On reflection, these can be narrowed down into four main stages

  1. Planning - Finding what will work best, the type of content that I will post and it's schedule.

  2. Designing - Design various types of content that relates to the goal of the campaign using Adobe Creative Cloud.

  3. Campaigning - The actual campaigning process, ensuring that all posts are released on time and that I am interacting with the audience.

  4. Reflection - Here I reflect on the outcomes, analyse the statistics and give feedback to the organisation in order to improve going forward.

Websites

Ecommerce Store

This is one of my most recent, personal projects, that was created in order to learn, and demonstate, how to build a functional, secure, ecommerce store.

The site uses SnipCart, an online store API that ensures all orders are encrypted and validated, removing the risk of fraudulent activity. The site is also created to be both responsive, and functional, so that users are able to easily find their desired product.

This site is made to be an example, so additional features and functionality could be added upon request.

UI/UX Design

Here are some examples of some prototypes of apps I've created during my time at university. These have all been designed on InVision, and have both had a full design process - from wireframes and mood boards to a functional prototype.

Moods.

Moods. is a progressive web application that was designed from wireframes, to prototype and finally to a functioning website.

It's purpose is to be a mental health companion app, which people can use to find support for anxiety, stress, sadness, anger, and happiness. It provides information and advice for those who may be suffering from the listed problems, and also features built-in applications to help them - such as a to-do app, meditation app, anxiety, and more.


Student Links

Student Links is a complete prototype that I designed on InVision, which was created for one of my assignments to create a unique application that would be useful.

The app is designed to offer support and guidance to university students in their city. It provides support for student living and health, locations of things to do and places students may need, social media competitions that encourage healthy living and budgeting, and a student forum, which allows students to offer suggestions for the app and find further support.
