About Me

I'm Josh Taylor, a young and enthusiastic university graduate who has now began a career in web design.

I pride myself on always ensuring to put as much effort as possible into everything I do - whether this be university, different job roles or personal experiences. I love to try new things as often as possible, which is why I always make the most out of every experience so that I can continue to grow and learn something new.

As of May 2021, I have completed my degree at De Montfort University, where I have achieved a First-Class Honours (BSc) in Media Production.

During my time on the course, I've learnt new skills and gained experience on a wide range of media forms, including front-end web design, social media marketing, video editing and production, photography, audio production and TV production.

What am I looking for?

Out of the modules that I specialised in during my final two years of my degree, I found that I enjoyed social media marketing the most, and often found that my campaigns proved to be very effective.

I have always found it important to follow a career that you enjoy, so would now like to follow a career in marketing. I believe my skills and work ethic will be effective for the marketing role, and this career be something that I will continue to be motivated in and want to develop in.

Experience

After graduating from university, I landed a job at Rawnet Digital Agency, an amazing digital design agency, as a Junior Web and Mobile Apps Tester. This role allows me to use my skills to help design real-world products for large clients.

My goal is to be able to progress within Rawnet and gain a wider variety of skills as I gain experience, allowing myself to have a successful career.

I understand the importance of working closely within a team for the best outcome, for both the clients and the team itself. The best work environment is one where everyone is comfortable, relaxed and getting along with one another.
