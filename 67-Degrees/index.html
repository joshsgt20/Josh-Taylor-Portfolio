FAQ'S

Why did you choose the name 67° Degrees?

Here at 67° Degrees, we heat all of our hot drinks to the temperature of, you guessed it, 67°C. If this isn't right for you, just tell one of our baristas - we have a kids temperature and can also make them extra hot!

Where are the locations of the 67° Degrees cafes?

67° Degrees are expanding, opening more cafes across England! For now, you can visit your local cafe in Leicester, Manchester, Birmingham, London, Milton Keynes and Leeds. We have plans of opening many more throughout this year alone, so stay updated with our newsletters to see when a cafe opens near you!
