Our Easter Selection

With the arrival of Easter, we bring to you a selection of new bakery items and some new drinks!

The food items include the release of freshly baked Hot Cross Buns, Simnel Cake, Carrot cake, rice crispy cakes and chocolate Easter cakes!

Make your drink more special with our new syrups, where you can try the new Marzipan Latte and Hot Cross Bun Latte!

Make your drink extra special!

At 67° Degrees, you can make your drink unique and special to you by simily asking the barista at the till! For example, you can have extra shots in your coffee, add whipped cream, choose one of our dairy alternative milks (Soya, Oat, Coconut, Almond and Coconut), have your drink iced or add any syrup of your choice!
