Who are We?


We are Global Heads.

A speciality global healthcare, recruitment and placement solutions company based which is based in London. We specialise in recruiting nurses, doctors and healthcare technicians for various work opportunities around the world and putting these people to work in both UK or US hospitals.

Our Mission
Our mission is to to mobilize, train and place highly skilled healthcare professionals from around the world, primarily travel nurses and health technicians, to help meeting staffing needs of global healthcare systems, particularly given the current climate in this area.

The leadership team at Global Heads have over 30 years of combined experience, working in Senior roles in National Health Services and with voluntary sector providers - meaning we understand your human resources challenges. We are therefore currently preparing a cadre of highly skilled and registered nurses for Nurses and Midwifery Council part 1 examinations, and will have them positioned and ready for health organisations to prepare them for part 2.

What do Global Heads do?

Our Vision
Is to be a leader in global health workforce mobilization and development, allowing us to give aid to health services around the world and ensure that these highly skilled health workers can find work.

In these extremely challenging times, there has never been a higher demand for health workers across the world to help deal with the stress from COVID-19. Therefore, our vision will help benefit health services across the world by being able to put highly trained Nurses and Health Technicians into paid employment in UK and US hospitals.

Global Health Development Programme

Prgramme Outline

Recent public health disasters, such as Ebola and the current COVID-19 pandemic, have both created challenges on a global scale, and had devastating impacts on our families, communities and health care systems. One thing that we have learnt from these public health emergancies, is that no one is immune to the effects of the problems that could occur in your city or country.

As the world begins to recover from the ongoing effects of COVID-19, some individuals and groups will be critical to inspire and build more resilient, globally-connected organisations that will allow health servicesand countries to work together, so that we can reduce the impact of any future public health emergencies.

    The course will be of interest to:

  • Anyone who wants to work in an NGO (internationally)

  • Health professionals who wish to enhance their skills;

  • Health pracitioners who wish to work internationally;

  • Anyone who has experience or certifications, such as public policy or business management, and wants to work in the public health sector;

  • Anyone who is working in Corporate Social Responsibility

  • NGO and social entrepreneurs.

This 8 week online global health course will provide participants with the knowledge, skills and tools to make significant contributions to address health issues - both domestically and globally. The course will consist of 8 modules, taught by a multi-discilinary team with extensive experience of working globally.

    During this 8 week course, you will:

  • Set your Global Health career goals;

  • Learn about global health systems and institutions;

  • Learn about different types of financing for global health projects;

  • Learn about global health innovations;

  • Learn how to develop a global health leader mindset and leadership skills.
